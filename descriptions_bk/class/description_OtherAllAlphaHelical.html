Superclass: Other all-alpha-helical domains

This superclass includes transcription factors (TFs) with a DNA-binding domain (DBD) composed exclusively of alpha-helices. The structure of these DBDs and their DNA-binding mechanism differ from other alpha-helix-based domains, such as HTH and basic domains.

It encompasses the heteromeric CCAAT-binding factors (also known as nuclear factor Y or NF-Y) and the High-Mobility Group (HMG) domain factors. Additionally, we expanded this superclass by introducing a new plant-specific class: EIL, which stands for Ethylene-Insensitive3 (EIN3)-like factors.
