Superclass: Beta-sheet binding to DNA domains

This superclass includes the AT-hook factors and TATA-binding protein classes, which interact with DNA primarily through single extended strands or beta-sheets, respectively, with a preference for the minor groove. AT-hook motifs are found in a diverse range of eukaryotic nuclear proteins.

Within this class, we incorporated the land plant-specific AT-hook motif nuclear-localized (AHL) transcription factors, which are not orthologous to HMGA and contain one or two AT-hook motifs. Additionally, we expanded the superclass by including the land plant-specific TCP factors.
