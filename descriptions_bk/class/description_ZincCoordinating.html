Superclass: Zinc-coordinating DNA binding domains

Transcription factors in this superclass have DNA-binding domains (DBDs) stabilized by one or more zinc ions. The nature of zinc coordination and the resulting DBD fold define nine classes in TFClass.

Among these, plants share two TF families with animals: C2H2 (from the C2H2 Zinc Finger Factors class) and C4-GATA-related (from the Other C4 Zinc Finger-Type Factors class). Additionally, we expanded this superclass by incorporating five plant-specific TF types: Squamosa Promoter Binding Proteins (SBP), DNA-binding One Zinc Finger (DOF), LATERAL ORGAN BOUNDARIES (LOB) DOMAIN (LBD), Arabidopsis LSH1 and Oryza G1 (ALOG), and Growth-Regulating Factors (GRF) proteins.
