Superclass: Basic domains

This superclass includes transcription factors (TFs) that bind DNA through a basic alpha-helix, typically inserted into the DNA major groove. It consists of two classes: the basic leucine zipper (bZIP) and basic helix-loop-helix (bHLH) TFs, both of which are found in most eukaryotes. These TFs are dimeric and interact with DNA by "pinching" it between two basic helices.
