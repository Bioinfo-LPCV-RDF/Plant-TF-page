Superclass: Yet-Undefined DBDs



Class: GRAS

The GRAS domain of SCARECROW-LIKE7 (SCL7) from rice was crystallized as a dimer (PDB ID: 5HYZ). Each monomer consists of a core region composed of both alpha-helices and beta-sheets, capped by an alpha-helical structure. The two cap regions form a candidate DNA-binding groove enriched with positive residues. DNA docking simulations within this groove predicted strong protein/DNA interactions, which were subsequently validated experimentally [86].

If confirmed at the structural level, this DNA-binding mechanism could potentially define a new superclass of transcription factors.
