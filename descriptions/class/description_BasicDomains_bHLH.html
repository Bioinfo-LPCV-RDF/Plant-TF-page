Superclass: Basic Domains



Class: bHLH

We have categorized the bHLH class into two distinct families:

  • Classical bHLH
  • BES/BZR (a plant-specific family)

BES/BZR transcription factors exhibit subtle but significant structural differences compared to classical bHLH proteins. While classical bHLH factors rely solely on a long basic helix to interact with DNA, the BES/BZR DNA-binding domains (DBDs) involve additional contacts: the loop between the two helices interacts with the minor groove, and the second helix—critical for dimerization—is notably shorter.
