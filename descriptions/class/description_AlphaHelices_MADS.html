Superclass: Alpha-helices exposed by beta-structures



Class: MADS

The MADS box factors class includes two families in plants: Type I and Type II. While the structure of the DBD/DNA complex has only been described in animals, the DNA-binding domain (DBD) structure of the Arabidopsis floral organ identity MADS transcription factor SEPALLATA3 was recently resolved without bound DNA (PDB ID: 7NB0). This study revealed no major structural differences compared to metazoan MADS proteins [81].
